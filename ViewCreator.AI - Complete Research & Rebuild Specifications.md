# ViewCreator.AI - Complete Research & Rebuild Specifications

## Executive Summary

ViewCreator.AI is a **multi-platform AI agent orchestration platform** designed for content creators to automate social media content creation, optimization, and publishing across YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, X (Twitter), and Facebook. The platform uses AI agents to handle research, creation, and publishing workflows automatically.

## 6. DESIGN SYSTEM

### Color Palette
- **Primary Blue**: #3B82F6 (used for CTAs, highlights, active states)
- **Red Accent**: #EF4444 (used for "The Old Way" comparisons)
- **Green Accent**: #10B981 (used for checkmarks, success states)
- **Background**: White/Light Gray
- **Text Primary**: #000000 (Black)
- **Text Secondary**: #6B7280 (Gray)

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Bold, large sans-serif (appears to be system font or similar to Inter)
- **Body Text**: Regular weight, readable sans-serif
- **Accent Text**: Blue for emphasis (e.g., "Virality" in hero)

### Components
- **Buttons**: 
  - Primary: Blue background, white text, rounded corners
  - Secondary: White background, blue border, blue text
  - Disabled: Gray background
- **Cards**: White background, subtle shadow, rounded corners
- **Badges**: Colored squares with numbers (1-12+) for feature/platform indicators
- **Comparison Cards**: Two-column layout with red/blue accent borders

### Spacing & Layout
- **Container**: Max-width ~1200px, centered
- **Section Padding**: Generous vertical padding (60-100px)
- **Card Padding**: 24-32px
- **Gap between elements**: 16-24px
