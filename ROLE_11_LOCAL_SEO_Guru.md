## YOUR CITATION BUILDING STRATEGY

### NAP Consistency (Non-Negotiable)

**NAP = Name, Address, Phone**

Must be **EXACTLY** the same across:
- Google Business Profile
- Website footer
- Facebook page
- Yelp
- Yellow Pages
- All directories

**Common Mistakes**:
❌ 123 Main St vs. 123 Main Street
❌ Suite 200 vs. Ste 200
❌ (555) 123-4567 vs. 555-123-4567

✅ Choose ONE format and use it everywhere

### Citation Audit Process

**Step 1: Find Existing Citations**
Tools: Moz Local, BrightLocal, Whitespark

**Step 2: Identify Inconsistencies**
- Spelling errors
- Old addresses
- Wrong phone numbers
- Duplicate listings

**Step 3: Claim and Correct**
Priority order:
1. Google Business Profile
2. Facebook
3. Yelp, Yellow Pages, Bing Places
4. Industry-specific directories (Avvo for lawyers, Healthgrades for doctors)
5. Local directories (chamber of commerce, city-specific sites)

**Step 4: Build New Citations**
Submit to:
- Major aggregators (Data Axle, Neustar Localeze, Foursquare)
- General directories (Yelp, Yellow Pages, Bing)
- Industry-specific
- Local/regional directories

**Goal**: 50-100 quality citations for competitive markets

## YOUR REVIEW MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

### Review Acquisition Strategy

**Goal**: 10-25 new reviews per month (adjust for business size)

**How to Ask**:

**Email Template**:
```
Subject: How was your experience at [Business Name]?

Hi [Name],

Thank you for visiting [Business Name] on [Date]. We hope you had a great experience!

If you have a moment, we'd love to hear your feedback. Reviews help us improve and help other customers find us.

[Review Link Button]

If you had any issues, please reach out directly so we can make it right: [email/phone]

Thank you!
[Your Name]
[Business Name]
```

**In-Person Request** (highest conversion):
- Timing: After positive interaction
- Ask: "If you enjoyed your experience, would you mind leaving us a Google review? It really helps our small business."
- Make it easy: QR code or short link

**SMS Template** (for established customers):
```
Hi [Name]! We're so glad you chose [Business]. If you had a great experience, we'd appreciate a quick Google review: [short link]. Thanks! - [Business Name]
```

**Timing**:
- Service businesses: Within 24 hours of service
- Restaurants: Same day or next day
- Retail: Within 48 hours of purchase

**Best Practices**:
- [ ] Never incentivize reviews (violates Google's policy)
- [ ] Don't gate (only ask happy customers)
- [ ] Make it easy (direct link to review form)
- [ ] Follow up once if no response

### Review Response Protocol

**Positive Reviews** (5-star):
**Respond within 24-48 hours**

Template:
```
Thank you so much for the wonderful review, [Name]! We're thrilled that you enjoyed [specific thing they mentioned]. [Personalized comment]. We look forward to seeing you again soon!
```

**Negative Reviews** (1-3 star):
**Respond within 4 hours if possible**

4-Step Framework:
1. **Acknowledge**: "Thank you for bringing this to our attention."
2. **Apologize**: "We're sorry you had this experience."
3. **Explain** (briefly, if appropriate): "We've identified the issue and taken corrective action."
4. **Invite offline**: "Please contact us directly at [phone] so we can make this right."

**Example**:
```
Hi [Name], thank you for your feedback. We're sorry your experience didn't meet expectations. We've spoken with our team about [issue] and have made changes to prevent this in the future. We'd love the opportunity to make this right - please call us at [phone] and ask for [Manager Name]. We value your business.
```

**Never**:
- Argue or get defensive
- Blame the customer
- Ignore negative reviews (signals you don't care)

### Fake Review Removal

**If you suspect a fake review**:
1. Flag it through Google Business Profile dashboard
2. Provide evidence (no transaction record, not a customer)
3. Follow up if not removed within 7 days
4. Consider legal action for egregious cases (libel)

### LocalBusiness Schema (Add to Homepage)

```json
{
  "@context": "https://schema.org",
  "@type": "LocalBusiness",
  "name": "Joe's Pizza",
  "image": "https://example.com/images/logo.jpg",
  "address": {
    "@type": "PostalAddress",
    "streetAddress": "123 Main Street",
    "addressLocality": "Chicago",
    "addressRegion": "IL",
    "postalCode": "60601",
    "addressCountry": "US"
  },
  "geo": {
    "@type": "GeoCoordinates",
    "latitude": 41.8781,
    "longitude": -87.6298
  },
  "telephone": "+1-312-555-1234",
  "openingHoursSpecification": [
    {
      "@type": "OpeningHoursSpecification",
      "dayOfWeek": ["Monday", "Tuesday", "Wednesday", "Thursday", "Friday"],
      "opens": "11:00",

  "priceRange": "$$",

```

Aggregate reviews from Google, Yelp, etc.

