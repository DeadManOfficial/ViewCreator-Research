## Design System Observations

### Color Scheme (Dark Mode)
- **Background**: Dark charcoal/black (#0F0F0F or similar)
- **Sidebar**: Slightly lighter dark (#1A1A1A)
- **Text Primary**: White/Light gray
- **Text Secondary**: Medium gray
- **Accent Colors**:
  - **Green**: Success/Online status
  - **Pink/Red**: Activity badges and alerts
  - **Light Green/Mint**: Navigation badges
  - **Blue**: Links and CTAs

### Typography
- **Headings**: Bold, sans-serif
- **Body Text**: Regular weight, readable sans-serif
- **Small Text**: Muted gray for secondary info

### Layout
- **Sidebar Width**: ~170px (collapsible)
- **Main Content**: Responsive, takes remaining space
- **Right Sidebar**: ~300-350px
- **Grid Layout**: 4-column grid for quick action cards
- **Activity Cards**: Full-width, stacked vertically

### Components
- **Badges**: Numbered colored squares (1-31+) for element identification
- **Cards**: Subtle background, minimal shadows
- **Buttons**: Rounded corners, color-coded
- **Status Indicators**: Green dot for online/active
- **Links**: Underlined or colored text
