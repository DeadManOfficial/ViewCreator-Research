## The Metricsmule Method
To achieve photorealistic results, every prompt MUST include:

1. **Camera & Lens**: Specific model (Canon R5, Sony A7IV, etc.)
2. **Lens Specs**: Focal length + aperture (85mm f/1.8)
3. **Technical Settings**: ISO, shutter speed, white balance
4. **Lighting**: Volumetric, practical, or natural with direction
5. **Color Science**: Real color grading terms (Kodak Portra, teal-orange)
6. **Texture Keywords**: RAW photography, high-end retouching, film grain
