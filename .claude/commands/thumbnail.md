## Thumbnail Psychology

### The 3-Second Rule
Viewers decide in 3 seconds. Your thumbnail must:
1. **Stop the scroll** - High contrast, bold colors
2. **Communicate value** - What will I learn/feel?
3. **Create curiosity** - But not reveal everything

### Proven Elements
- **Faces**: Extreme expressions (shock, joy, anger)
- **Text**: 3 words max, huge font
- **Contrast**: Light on dark or dark on light
- **Rule of Thirds**: Key elements on intersection points
- **Before/After**: Transformation appeal
- **Numbers**: Specific numbers outperform vague claims

### Platform Specifications
- YouTube: 1280x720 (16:9)
- TikTok Cover: 1080x1920 (9:16)
- Instagram: 1080x1080 (1:1) or 1080x1350 (4:5)
