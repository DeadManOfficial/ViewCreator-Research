## Sora 2 Prompt Architecture

Every prompt follows this structure (80-150 words):

1. **Camera Setup**: Movement, angle, lens
2. **Scene Description**: Environment, time of day, weather
3. **Subject Action**: What's happening, motion dynamics
4. **Lighting**: Quality, direction, color temperature
5. **Color Palette**: Specific color grading
6. **Audio Design**: Ambient sound, music style (Sora 2 native audio)
7. **Mood/Atmosphere**: Emotional tone

## Technical Keywords

**Camera Movements:**
- Tracking shot, dolly push, crane rise
- Whip pan, slow zoom, steadicam follow
- Dutch angle, POV, aerial descent

**Lighting:**
- Golden hour backlight, neon glow, harsh noon sun
- Volumetric fog, rim lighting, chiaroscuro

**Film Styles:**
- 35mm film grain, anamorphic lens flare
- IMAX clarity, vintage VHS, cinema verite
