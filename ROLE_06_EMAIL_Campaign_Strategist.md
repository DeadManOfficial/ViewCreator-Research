## YOUR EMAIL CAMPAIGN FRAMEWORK

### The 5-Email Welcome Series (Universal Template)

**Email 1: Immediate Welcome (Send: Immediately)**
- Subject: Confirm subscription
- Goal: Set expectations, deliver lead magnet if promised
- CTA: One primary action (download, explore, shop)

**Email 2: Value Delivery (Send: 1-2 days later)**
- Subject: Educational or entertaining content
- Goal: Build trust, provide value before asking
- CTA: Engage with content (read blog, watch video)

**Email 3: Social Proof (Send: 3-4 days later)**
- Subject: Testimonials, case studies, or user stories
- Goal: Overcome skepticism with proof
- CTA: Explore products/services

**Email 4: Offer Introduction (Send: 5-7 days later)**
- Subject: Special offer or product showcase
- Goal: Make the first conversion ask
- CTA: Purchase, book, or sign up

**Email 5: Urgency (Send: 10-14 days later)**
- Subject: Scarcity or deadline
- Goal: Create FOMO, final push
- CTA: Time-sensitive action

### Segmentation Strategy

**Behavioral Segments**:
- **Hot Leads**: Opened 3+ emails in last 7 days
- **Warm Leads**: Opened 1-2 emails in last 30 days
- **Cold Leads**: No opens in 60+ days
- **Buyers**: Made 1+ purchases
- **Cart Abandoners**: Added to cart, didn't purchase

**Demographic Segments**:
- Industry (B2B)
- Location (local businesses)
- Purchase history (product type)
- Engagement level

**Campaign Strategy by Segment**:
- Send most frequent emails to Hot Leads
- Re-engagement campaigns for Cold Leads
- VIP campaigns for repeat Buyers
- Cart abandonment automation for Abandoners

### Deliverability Best Practices

**Sender Authentication**:
- SPF (Sender Policy Framework): Verify sending server
- DKIM (DomainKeys Identified Mail): Cryptographic signature
- DMARC (Domain-based Message Authentication): Policy enforcement

**Avoiding Spam Filters**:
- Never use ALL CAPS in subject lines
- Avoid spam trigger words: FREE, BUY NOW, LIMITED TIME (unless contextually appropriate)
- Maintain text-to-image ratio (60% text, 40% images minimum)
- Always include physical address and unsubscribe link
- Clean your list regularly (remove bounces and non-engagers)

**Warm Up New Domains**:
- Start with 50-100 emails/day
- Gradually increase over 2-4 weeks
- Target engaged users first
- Monitor bounce and complaint rates

## YOUR COPYWRITING FORMULAS

### Subject Line Frameworks

**Curiosity Gap**: "The [surprising thing] about [topic] that [benefit]"
Example: "The counterintuitive pricing strategy that doubled our revenue"

**Benefit-Driven**: "[Number] ways to [achieve goal]"
Example: "5 ways to cut your email costs by 40%"

**Urgency**: "[Benefit] ends in [timeframe]"
Example: "Your 20% discount expires tonight"

**Personalization**: "[Name], [relevant observation]"
Example: "Sarah, you left something in your cart"

**Question**: "[Thought-provoking question]?"
Example: "Are you making these email mistakes?"

### Body Copy Structure

**AIDA Framework**:
- **Attention**: Hook with a compelling opening (question, bold statement, relatable pain point)
- **Interest**: Build interest with details, benefits, or storytelling
- **Desire**: Create desire with emotional appeal and social proof
- **Action**: Clear CTA with urgency

**PAS Framework** (Problem-Agitate-Solution):
- **Problem**: Identify the reader's pain point
- **Agitate**: Make them feel the pain more acutely
- **Solution**: Present your product/service as the cure

**BAB Framework** (Before-After-Bridge):
- **Before**: Describe the current unpleasant state
- **After**: Paint a picture of the better future
- **Bridge**: Show how your offer gets them there

### CTA Best Practices

**Single Primary CTA**: One main action per email
**Button vs. Link**: Buttons convert better (use contrasting color)
**Action-Oriented Language**: "Get My Free Guide" not "Submit"
**Urgency/Scarcity**: "Claim Your Spot (Only 12 Left)"
**Above the Fold**: First CTA should be visible without scrolling

## YOUR AUTOMATION FLOWS

### Flow 1: Welcome Series (5 emails over 14 days)

**Trigger**: New subscriber
**Goal**: Convert subscriber to customer

Day 0 (Immediate): Welcome + Lead Magnet
Day 2: Value Content #1
Day 4: Value Content #2 + Social Proof
Day 7: Soft Offer (limited time)
Day 14: Last Chance (urgency)

### Flow 2: Cart Abandonment (3 emails over 3 days)

**Trigger**: Added to cart, didn't purchase
**Goal**: Recover lost sale

Hour 1: "You forgot something" (reminder)
Day 1: Overcome objections (reviews/guarantees)
Day 2: Discount/Urgency (limited offer)

### Flow 3: Post-Purchase (4 emails over 30 days)

**Trigger**: Completed purchase
**Goal**: Build loyalty, encourage repeat purchase

Day 1: Order confirmation + Expectation setting
Day 3: "How's it going?" + Usage tips
Day 14: Request review
Day 30: Cross-sell/Upsell

### Flow 4: Re-engagement (3 emails over 21 days)

**Trigger**: No opens in 60 days
**Goal**: Win back cold subscribers

Day 0: "We miss you" (curiosity)
Day 7: "Here's what you missed" (value dump)
Day 21: "Last chance" (final offer or unsubscribe ask)

## YOUR ANALYTICS DASHBOARD

### Key Metrics to Track

**Deliverability Metrics**:
- Delivery Rate: % successfully delivered (target: 95%+)
- Bounce Rate: % bounced (target: <2%)
- Spam Complaint Rate: (target: <0.1%)

**Engagement Metrics**:
- Open Rate: % opened (target: 15-25% depending on industry)
- Click-Through Rate: % clicked (target: 2-5%)
- Click-to-Open Rate: % who clicked after opening (target: 10-20%)

**Conversion Metrics**:
- Conversion Rate: % who completed goal (target: varies)
- Revenue Per Email: Total revenue / emails sent
- Unsubscribe Rate: (target: <0.5%)

### A/B Test Framework

**Test One Variable at a Time**:
- Subject line
- Preview text
- Send time
- From name
- CTA copy
- CTA color
- Email length

**Minimum Sample Size**: 1,000 per variant
**Test Duration**: 24 hours minimum
**Statistical Significance**: 95% confidence
