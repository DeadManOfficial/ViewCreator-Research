### The Four Main Areas of a Prompt
The **Gemini for Google Workspace Prompting Guide** emphasizes a simple yet powerful structure for any effective prompt, ensuring clarity and completeness.

1.  **Persona:** Define the role the AI should adopt (e.g., "You are a senior marketing analyst"). This sets the tone, expertise, and perspective of the response.
2.  **Task:** State the specific action required (e.g., "Draft an executive summary," "Analyze this data," "Create a content plan"). This should be a clear and direct command.
3.  **Context:** Provide the necessary background information, data, or constraints the AI needs to perform the task effectively.
4.  **Format:** Specify the desired structure of the output (e.g., "in a markdown table," "as a bulleted list," "in a professional email format").

### Persona Manipulation and Jailbreaking
Several documents touch on advanced techniques for persona manipulation, including "jailbreaks" designed to bypass standard AI safety guardrails or elicit more unconstrained, creative, or detailed responses. These prompts often work by establishing a strong, fictional context or persona that makes the desired output seem natural within that narrative. While powerful, these techniques should be used with an understanding of the AI's terms of service.

This pack provides a master template and specialized prompts for generating professional headshots across various industries (e.g., Executive, Legal, Creative, Healthcare). **Best Practices for Identity-Preserving Edits:**

-   **Be Explicit with Constraints:** The prompts strictly forbid common AI alterations like face-slimming, plastic-skin smoothing, or changing facial structure. The instruction "DO NOT change my face" is a common and critical component.
-   **Request Minimal, Specific Changes:** The prompts isolate the changes to the environment, such as lighting, wardrobe, and background, while explicitly demanding the subject's identity be kept the same.
-   **Use Technical Language:** Instead of asking to "make me look more professional," the prompts specify "professional studio lighting," "head-and-shoulders crop," and "shallow depth of field."
-   **Iterate with Micro-Adjustments:** The pack includes prompts for fine-tuning, such as "Fix only the eyes to look natural and symmetric" or "Reduce skin smoothing; restore natural pores and texture."
