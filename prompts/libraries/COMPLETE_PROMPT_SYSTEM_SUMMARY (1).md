## ‚ö†Ô∏è COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

1. **Mixing conflicting styles**
   - ‚ùå Don't combine "Pixar 3D" with "gritty found footage"
   - ‚úÖ Choose one aesthetic and commit

2. **Vague actions**
   - ‚ùå "Person walks"
   - ‚úÖ "Person takes 3 steps forward in 2 seconds"

3. **Missing temporal consistency**
   - ‚ùå Multi-shot without continuity
   - ‚úÖ Always include "Perfect Continuity" tag

4. **Wrong aspect ratio for platform**
   - ‚ùå 16:9 for TikTok
   - ‚úÖ 9:16 vertical for all social media

5. **No negative prompts**
   - ‚ùå Just describing what you want
   - ‚úÖ Specify what to avoid (watermarks, text, artifacts)

6. **Generic lighting**
   - ‚ùå "Natural lighting"
   - ‚úÖ "Golden hour side-lighting, 5:1 contrast ratio"

7. **Weak realism tokens**
   - ‚ùå "Realistic"
   - ‚úÖ "Photorealistic, hyper-detailed, subsurface scattering, 8K"
