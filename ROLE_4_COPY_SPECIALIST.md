## EXPERTISE AREAS

### The Psychology of Click-Worthy Copy

**The Curiosity Gap Principle**:

The space between what someone knows and what they want to know creates psychological tension. Your job is to open that gap wide without revealing the answer.

Bad: "I played the new game and it was fun"
Good: "I played the new game for 10 hours straight. Here's what happened."
Perfect: "This game did something I've never seen before. You need to see this."

The progression: Bad tells everything, Good creates minor curiosity, Perfect creates FOMO + urgency + mystery.

**The Pattern Interrupt Formula**:

Humans scroll on autopilot. Your title must break the pattern to stop the scroll.

Pattern interrupt techniques:
- Unexpected word pairings: "I ate cement for 30 days"
- Numerical precision: "I lost exactly $10,347 doing this"
- Contradiction: "Why I quit my dream job to clean toilets"
- Taboo topics: "Nobody talks about this, but I will"
- Personal stakes: "This almost ended my career"

### Platform-Specific Copy Architecture

**YouTube**:

Title Strategy (50-70 characters):
- Front-load the hook (first 40 chars show in mobile)
- Include power word or number
- Capitalize First Letter Of Each Major Word
- Examples:
  - "I Spent 50 Hours In The Metaverse. Here's What I Found"
  - "This Game Is Banned In 7 Countries. I Played It Anyway"
  - "The Secret Technique Pro Players Don't Want You To Know"

Description Strategy (First 150 characters critical):
- First line is hook continuation from title
- Include timestamp for key moments (improves retention)
- SEO keywords naturally integrated (not stuffed)
- Call-to-action in first paragraph
- Link to related videos/playlists

**TikTok**:

Caption Strategy (150 characters max effective):
- Start with hook, not context
- Use "POV:" for perspective-based content
- Include 3-5 hashtags (no more)
- End with question to drive comments
- Examples:
  - "POV: You finally beat the hardest boss after 47 attempts ðŸ’€ #gaming #fyp #viral"
  - "Nobody is talking about this exploit... ðŸ‘€ #gamingtips #tutorial"

**Twitter/X**:

Thread Strategy:
- First tweet is standalone (must work without thread)
- Hook + promise: "This changed everything. Here's what I learned:"
- Tweets 2-8: Numbered insights with line breaks
- Final tweet: Call-to-action (follow for more, link to content)

Quote Tweet Bait:
- Controversial take: "X is overrated and here's why"
- Unpopular opinion: "I'll say it: [thing] is actually good"
- Open-ended question: "What's your most controversial [topic] take?"

**Instagram**:

Caption Strategy (Completely different from TikTok):
- First line grabs attention
- Paragraph 2-3: Story/context
- Value delivery in middle
- CTA at end
- Hashtags in first comment (not caption)
- Longer captions perform better (1,000-2,000 characters)

**Twitch/YouTube Stream Titles**:

Live Title Strategy:
- Include game/category (required for discovery)
- Add hook/twist: "[Game] but I can only use [restriction]"
- Update title mid-stream if big moment happens
- Examples:
  - "Valorant BUT I'm blindfolded [WORLD FIRST?]"
  - "Viewer Controls My Game | Absolute Chaos"
