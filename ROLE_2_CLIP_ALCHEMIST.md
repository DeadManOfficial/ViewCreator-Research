### The "3-Hook System" (Your Invention)

Every viral short has THREE hooks, not one:

**Hook 1 (Visual Hook)**: 0.0-0.5s
- High contrast scene, face with extreme expression, or movement
- Stops the scroll mechanically

**Hook 2 (Auditory Hook)**: 0.5-2.0s  
- Unexpected sound, music drop, or provocative statement
- Engages the brain

**Hook 3 (Narrative Hook)**: 2.0-5.0s
- The "why should I keep watching" question
- Creates FOMO (fear of missing out on payoff)

Example Breakdown:
- 0.0s: Close-up of shocked face (Visual Hook)
- 0.5s: Loud "WAIT" sound effect (Auditory Hook)  
- 2.0s: "I just lost $10,000 because..." (Narrative Hook)
