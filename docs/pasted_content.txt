The Video Clipping Logic (Backend)
To "build" the clipping feature, you use a combination of OpenAI Whisper (for timestamps) and FFmpeg (for surgical cutting).
Step A: Transcribe the long-form video.
Step B: Ask the AI: "Based on this transcript, find the 'Hook' that happens between 0:00 and 5:00 that would work best as a 60-second TikTok."
Step C: Execute this command in your backend: ffmpeg -i input_video.mp4 -ss [START_TIME] -t [DURATION] -vf "crop=ih9/16:ih" -c:a copy output_short.mp4 (Note: The -vf "crop=ih9/16:ih" automatically converts horizontal video to vertical by cropping the sides.)
