Based on the screenshots provided, ViewCreator.AI is a comprehensive content creation and social media management platform that leverages AI to automate various tasks.

1. AI Agent Configuration
Custom Agent Creation: You can create and name specific AI agents.

Behavioral Programming: You can define an agent's "Mission & Instructions" to control its tone, goals, and specific constraints, effectively giving it a unique persona or job description.

Brand Alignment: Agents can be assigned a specific "Brand Profile" to ensure they adhere to a consistent brand voice.

Workflow Automation: Agents can be set up in a visual workflow (e.g., Start -> Plan -> Video -> Publish) to automate multi-step content creation processes.

Thumbnails: Generate high-CTR thumbnails using AI.

Titles: Create SEO-optimized video titles.

Chapters: Automatically generate video timestamps.

Transcripts: Convert video audio into text transcripts.

Channel Management: Tools to publish/schedule videos, view analytics, manage metadata, and moderate comments.

Idea Spark: Generate captions and hooks before publishing.

Management: Schedule posts/reels, moderate comments with AI, and view profile insights.

Facebook Studio:

Content Generator: Create engaging captions and hooks.

Management: View pages, publish content (text, photo, video), view analytics, edit page profiles, and moderate comments.

TikTok Studio:

Captions: Generate captions designed to boost engagement.

Hashtags: Find trending hashtags to maximize reach.

Video Ideas: Generate viral video concepts based on trends.

Trending Sounds: Discover trending music and audio.

Hook Generator: Create hooks to grab attention in the first 3 seconds.

X (Twitter) Studio:

Image Transformation: Transform existing images.

Video Creation: The interface includes a toggle for "Video," suggesting AI video generation capabilities alongside image tools.

Post Scheduling: A unified "Post" section to design and publish content across platforms.
