# Official Sora 2 Content Policies

## Source
https://openai.com/policies/creating-sora-videos-in-line-with-our-policies/
Last updated: December 5, 2025

## Key Policy Categories

### 1. OpenAI Usage Policies
All users must comply with:
- Usage Policies
- Service Terms
- Terms of Use

### 2. Sora Distribution Guidelines

Content deemed inappropriate may be removed from:
- Sora Feed
- User galleries
- Side characters
- Other sharing platforms

### Prohibited Content Categories:

1. **Sexual Content**
   - Graphic sexual content

2. **Violence & Gore**
   - Graphic violence
   - Content promoting violence

3. **Extremism & Hate**
   - Extremist propaganda
   - Hateful content

4. **Political Content**
   - Targeted political persuasion

5. **Self-Harm & Mental Health**
   - Content promoting/depicting self-harm
   - Content promoting disordered eating
   - Unhealthy dieting or exercise behaviors
   - Content glorifying depression

6. **Appearance-Based Harm**
   - Appearance-based critiques or comparisons
   - Bullying content

7. **Dangerous Activities**
   - Dangerous challenges likely to be imitated by minors
   - Promotion of age-restricted goods or activities
   - Illegal drugs or harmful substances

8. **Low Quality/Spam**
   - Low quality content where primary purpose is engagement bait

9. **Likeness & Deepfakes**
   - Content recreating likeness of living public figures without consent
   - Deceased public figures in contexts where likeness not permitted

10. **Intellectual Property**
    - Content that may infringe on IP rights of others

### 3. Reporting Mechanism
- Users can report violations immediately
- All violations reviewed for compliance
